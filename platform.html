A Platform that solved a complex problem

From personal experience I believe that Upwork has solved a complex business problem. Their platform brings masses of freelancers and clients together. It reduces the amount of time and effort spent by a freelancer on marketing significantly. It also makes it easier for clients to find freelancers for a variety of projects. I would, however, change the approach at this stage as the platform has grown massively over the past few years and has become "bottom heavy", for lack of a better word.

I would incorporate some form of vetting procedure for new freelancers wishing to join the platform. Such freelancers can them be classified as either beginner, intermediate or expert. Clients can then require a freelancer with a certain level of experience instead of the current system which group projects by value. This would prevent beginners from entering proposals on more complicated projects and vice versa.
