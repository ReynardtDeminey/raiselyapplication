001 : Why am I a good fit?

Why am I a good fit? That's always a good queation. I can tell you I am a quick learner or that I am excellent at problem solving. Or maybe that I am a great analytical thinker. But, I think for this position, although not irrelevant, it is not that important. I think the most important quality to possess for this position is the ability to help people. That is precisely the reason I am a good fit. I have a passion for helping people. Nothing makes me more happy than seeing someone succeed. I cultivated this passion during my years as a legal professional and I would jump at the opportunity to combine this passion with my other passion, technology. I would love the opportunity to help people solve their problems and see them succeed.
